How it works

Anomalies are data points that deviate significantly from the rest of the dataset. Detecting them helps in identifying fraud, equipment failure, or sudden market shifts.

Isolation Forest
An ensemble algorithm that isolates anomalies by randomly selecting a feature and then randomly selecting a split value.
Z-Score
Statistical method that measures how many standard deviations a data point is from the mean.
